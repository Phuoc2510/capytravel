# Travel Booking Web
## System's Purpose:
- To provide a platform where people can easily book flights with convenient operations and quick service.
- To manage flights schedule,processing booking orders.
## Who Uses the System?
- **Customers:** Booking flights, checking flights.
- **Administrators:** Content Management, Customer Service Management and Marketing and Promotion
## Features:
### 1.User Management:
- **Account Creation and Access:** Users can sign up, log in, and recover forgotten passwords.
- **Profile Settings:** Users have the ability to view and modify their profile details.
### 2.Flights Searching:
- Find flights by entering location, dates, number of guests, number of seats,choosing seat class.
### 3.Booking:
- Easy booking by choosing the airline, choose the flight with the appropriate time, select departure and return ticket type
- Secure payment methods like credit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers.
### 4.Content Management (Administrators): 
- **Add/Update/Delete:** Updating and editing information about flights.
### 5.Financial Reporting (Administrators): 
- Tracking revenue, expenses, and other financial metrics.
